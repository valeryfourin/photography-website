Hello!

My name is Kate Novitska. I'm a beginning photographer and I need you! I'm looking for enthusiastic people with interesting ideas to bring them to life.

Photography is my passion. I guarantee you an amusing pastime and gorgeous photos afterwards. If interested, go take a look at some of my work. Hope you like it!

I adore travelling, trying new things and getting experience and most of all I value art. That's why I want to be there for you to create memories from events you would like to remember.

I am in a constant search for personal and professional growth, so I will make sure, that you get the best shots using only natural editing.

As for the subjects I can do different types of photoshoots: personal, child photoshoots, school events, parties, family moments, love stories, weddings, christening etc. I'm planning to extend my portfolio and apply the knowledge and potential to reproduce your memories into photos.

Got interested? Please contact me by any comfortable way. I would be really glad to work with you!
